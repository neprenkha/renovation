# Catalog V5 Owner Decisions Required

The V5 public catalogs use conservative wording until the following specifications are confirmed:

1. Floor-tile size and RM allowance for Basic, Standard and Better Finish.
2. Tile-skirting inclusion, height and areas.
3. Basic ceiling or exposed-roof underside treatment.
4. Standard ceiling area, board type, framing, joint treatment and access panel.
5. Better Finish ceiling board, access panel and cornice scope.
6. Complete door-set inclusion and door type for each package.
7. Complete window inclusion, frame system, glass and hardware.
8. Included electrical quantities and wiring method for each package.
9. Included plumbing quantities and overlap with the Laundry / Yard Rough-In Set.
10. Basic bathroom WC: one-piece or two-piece, brand and model.
11. Standard bathroom WC: one-piece or two-piece, brand and model.
12. Better bathroom WC: one-piece or two-piece, brand and model.
13. Toilet-door material, frame, lockset, hinges, handle, stopper and finish.
14. Bathroom wall-tile height for each package.
15. Bathroom floor/wall tile category, size and RM allowance.
16. Exhaust-fan point and fan inclusion for each bathroom package.
17. Mirror and accessory inclusion for each bathroom package.
18. Sink, tap and plumbing-connection inclusion for table-top and wet-kitchen sets.
19. Base cabinet, wall cabinet or aluminium-cabinet inclusion in wet-kitchen sets.
20. Primer/sealer, internal coats, ceiling coats, exterior paint and door/frame paint system.

## Temporary V5 treatment

- Tile sizes, allowances, models, quantities and routes must be stated in the official quotation.
- Items not clearly included remain excluded or separately quoted.
- One toilet door set is included in the bathroom add-on, but its material and ironmongery must be stated.
- The bathroom WC type and model must be stated in the official quotation.
- Main extension packages include normal opening formation and making good only; complete doors, windows, aluminium and glass systems are excluded unless listed.
