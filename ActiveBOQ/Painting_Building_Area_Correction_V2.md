# Painting Building Area Correction V2

## Purpose

Owner corrected the paint scope basis again.

The previous paint correction used only the known major internal areas:

- living hall;
- kitchen;
- master bedroom.

That is still not enough if the customer is asking for painting based on the full building area.

## Owner correction

Customer paint wording:

`Cat (Vanilla latte/timeless)`

Building paint area basis:

`15240mm x 6096mm`

House level:

`2 storey`

If including porch/front area up to front fence:

`22860mm x 6096mm`

Owner note:

The 15240mm x 6096mm building area does not fully count the internal room walls, bathroom walls, and staircase wall area. Because the house is 2 storey, these internal walls must not be ignored if the paint scope is whole-house internal painting.

## Internal wall / stair allowance

The 4380.15sqft minimum still does not fully cover internal room walls, bathroom walls, and staircase wall area.

Use a provisional internal wall/stair allowance until detailed internal wall dimensions are extracted.

Practical provisional allowance:

`30 percent of net wall area`

| Item | Formula | Result |
|---|---|---:|
| Internal wall/stair provisional allowance | 2380.08sqft x 30 percent | 714.02sqft |
| Revised rough paint area | 4380.15sqft + 714.02sqft | 5094.17sqft |

## Recommendation

Use RM93000 as the safer revised customer estimate for the 2-storey building-area paint basis with provisional internal wall/stair allowance.

Use RM91000 only if painting is restricted to known building envelope/major areas and internal walls/stair walls are excluded.

Use RM97000 or remeasure first if whole-house paint is wider, includes many internal partitions, or includes porch/front fence area.

## Front porch / fence note

If the scope includes porch and front area up to the fence using 22860mm x 6096mm, do not merge it silently into the 15240mm x 6096mm building paint figure.

That front/porch/fence scope must be measured or added as a separate provisional line.
