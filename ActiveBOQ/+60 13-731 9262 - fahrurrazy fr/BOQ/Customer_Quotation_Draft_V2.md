## Main assumptions

- Living hall SPC area estimated at 180 sqft.
- SPC installed over existing tile.
- No floor levelling included unless site check shows uneven floor.
- No existing tile removal included.
- SPC delivery treated as included in installation labour.
- SPC material uses old AdditionalMaterials price with +10% allowance.
- Foam is treated as included if selected SPC product from PDF no. 7-12 has foam/underlay attached.
- Cabinet sizes are logical floorplan estimates and must be confirmed by final measurement/drawing.
- Electrical is based on known customer counts plus logical estimate for missing points.
- Painting is affected-zone estimate, not whole-house painting unless later confirmed.

## Exclusions / subject to confirmation

- Final cabinet shop drawing changes beyond agreed design.
- Premium hardware or branded accessories not listed.
- Appliance supply.
- Major wall alteration or structural works.
- DB upgrade, earthing correction or concealed route difficulty not visible from floor plan.
- Additional plumbing route if actual site route is longer or blocked.
- Delivery or access surcharge for other trades if later imposed.
- Final colour/model selection.
