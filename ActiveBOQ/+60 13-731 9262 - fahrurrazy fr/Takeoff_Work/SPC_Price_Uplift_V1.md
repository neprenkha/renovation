## Purpose

Owner confirmed that `AdditionalMaterials/SPC & Vynil` may contain old prices, but the old price can still be used for cost estimate by applying a logical 5% to 10% uplift.

This file records base, +5% and +10% material cost options for the current ActiveBOQ SPC flooring scope.

## Uplift rule

Because the price may be old, use these options:

| Option | Use |
|---|---|
| Base | Raw price reference, useful for lowest cost check. |
| +5% | Moderate price age allowance. |
| +10% | Safer estimate where price may have increased. |

Owner instruction: 5% to 10% yearly/logical increase can be used for cost estimate. For safer costing, use +10% unless owner chooses otherwise.
