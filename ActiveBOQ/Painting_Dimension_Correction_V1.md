# Painting Dimension Correction V1

## Purpose

Owner asked whether house painting was already calculated correctly from the floor plan.

Answer: no. The previous estimate used a rough affected-zone allowance:

`900 sqft x RM1.80 = RM1,620.00`

That was not a proper drawing-dimension paint takeoff.

## Correction basis

This correction uses the current known drawing dimensions already provided by owner:

| Area | Dimension |
|---|---:|
| Living hall | 9755mm x 6096mm |
| Kitchen | 6096mm x 5792mm |
| Master bedroom | 5792mm x 6096mm |

Provisional wall height used:

`10ft`

Opening/window/door deduction used:

`15 percent`

Painting rate used from previous working estimate:

`RM1.80/sqft`

## Wall painting calculation

| Area | Feet size | Gross wall area formula | Gross wall area |
|---|---:|---|---:|
| Living hall | 32.00ft x 20.00ft | 2 x (32.00 + 20.00) x 10ft | 1,040.09sqft |
| Kitchen | 20.00ft x 19.00ft | 2 x (20.00 + 19.00) x 10ft | 780.05sqft |
| Master bedroom | 19.00ft x 20.00ft | 2 x (19.00 + 20.00) x 10ft | 780.05sqft |

Gross wall total:

`2,600.20sqft`

Less 15 percent openings:

`2,600.20 x 0.85 = 2,210.17sqft`

## Ceiling painting / finish allowance

## Important notes

- This correction covers the known major areas with dimensions currently provided: living hall, kitchen and master bedroom.
- If painting scope is whole house and other rooms/corridor/stair/utility areas are also included, add them separately after extracting their dimensions.
- If ceiling paint is included inside plaster ceiling package and owner wants to exclude separate ceiling painting, remove the ceiling/finish allowance before final quote.
- Final site measurement and confirmed painting scope still control final price.
