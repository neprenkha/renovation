# Customer Estimate Answer V1

## Purpose

Owner asked what amount can be given when the customer asks for an estimate for the original ActiveBOQ scope.

## Recommended owner answer

Give the customer a provisional estimate of:

`Around RM45,000`

This is the safer customer-facing estimate based on the current rough scope and markup-aware draft.

## Acceptable range for owner use

| Use case | Amount |
|---|---:|
| Minimum margin-protected entry figure | RM43,000 |
| Recommended estimate to tell customer | RM45,000 |
| Higher risk / safer buffer figure | RM47,000 |

## Customer-facing wording

Based on the floor plan and the scope you listed, the estimated budget is around **RM45,000**.

This includes kitchen cabinet works, TV cabinet, master bedroom cabinet with make-up table, shoe cabinet, plaster ceiling, electrical wiring allowance, washing-machine plumbing relocation, dining feature wall, SPC flooring to living hall and painting to affected areas.

This is still a provisional estimate. Final quotation will depend on site measurement, final cabinet drawing, selected material/colour, exact wiring point count, plumbing route and final finishing confirmation.

Do not offer RM39,000 as customer estimate unless owner intentionally wants to give a cost-only/no-margin rough figure.

Use RM45,000 as the normal estimate.
