## Corrected rule for this ActiveBOQ job

For SPC flooring over existing tile floor:

1. Check the NII Floor product PDF item no. 7-12 before deciding foam status.
2. If the selected SPC product already includes foam/underlay or attached foam backing, do not add a separate foam material line.
3. Record foam as `FOAM INCLUDED IN PRODUCT SPEC`.
4. If later a selected SPC product does not include foam, add separate foam/underlay only when a price source is available.
5. Do not invent a foam/underlay price.
