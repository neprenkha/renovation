# V5 Customer Answer Sheet

Use this sheet when customer asks what is included. Answer by specification and tier, not by brand. Brand/model is confirmed only in the official quotation.

## Basic

Basic is for normal extension with practical finish.

Typical inclusion:

- economy ceramic floor tile;
- tile skirting about 4 inch / 100mm;
- plain flat plaster ceiling board;
- one standard single-leaf door set up to about 3ft x 7ft where layout requires it;
- one standard aluminium framed window up to about 4ft x 3ft where layout requires it;
- up to 1 light point with switch and 2 x 13A socket points for normal extension up to about 150 sqft;
- back/kitchen/yard/laundry only: up to 1 cold-water point, 1 waste outlet and 1 floor trap if route is practical;
- surface preparation, sealer/primer where required and two coats emulsion paint.

## Standard

Standard improves finish and allowance while keeping the same structure basis.

Typical inclusion:

- better ceramic or entry-level vitrified floor tile;
- tile skirting about 4 inch / 100mm;
- flat plaster ceiling board with smoother joint treatment and access panel where required;
- one standard single-leaf door set where layout requires it;
- one aluminium framed window up to about 5ft x 4ft where layout requires it;
- up to 2 light points with switch and 3 x 13A socket points for normal extension up to about 150 sqft;
- back/kitchen/yard/laundry only: Basic plumbing plus 1 washing-machine point or outdoor tap where listed;
- smoother finishing and standard paint range by quotation.

## Better Finish

Better Finish is for upgraded visible finish, tile tier, ceiling design option, point allowance and bathroom/wet-area allowance.

Typical inclusion:

- upgraded vitrified or approved premium-category floor tile;
- tile skirting about 4 inch / 100mm;
- simple design plaster ceiling may be included, such as tray, L-box or feature ceiling in agreed area;
- one upgraded door set/finish where listed;
- one larger or upgraded aluminium framed window up to about 6ft x 4ft where layout requires it;
- up to 3 light/downlight points with switching and 4 x 13A socket points for normal extension up to about 150 sqft;
- back/kitchen/yard/laundry only: Standard plumbing plus one utility-sink or outdoor-tap point where listed;
- premium paint range or upgraded finish where listed.

## Bathroom

Basic bathroom includes standard two-piece close-coupled WC set, bidet spray, basin, basin tap/trap, cold-water shower set, floor trap, practical waste route, waterproofing, economy ceramic tile, wall tile to agreed height, toilet door up to about 2.5ft x 7ft and 1 light point.

Standard bathroom upgrades WC, tap, basin, shower, waterproofing and tile allowance where listed.

Better Finish bathroom may include premium one-piece WC combo set, upgraded tap, basin, shower, tile and accessory allowance where listed.

Excluded unless listed: water heater, shower screen, LED mirror, premium mirror cabinet, premium vanity cabinet, premium accessories, major sewer/manhole relocation, soil-stack relocation and long concealed pipe route.

## Wet kitchen

Wet Kitchen Set A/B/C may include concrete/tiled table top for selected straight-run length, sink opening, 1 standard kitchen sink, 1 standard kitchen tap and cold-water/waste/drainage connection to practical nearby route when listed.

Base cabinet, wall cabinet, aluminium cabinet, quartz, granite, solid surface, hood, hob, appliances, premium sink/tap and long concealed plumbing route are quoted separately unless listed.

## Brand answer

Brand/model will be confirmed in the official quotation. The catalog states specification and allowance tier first so the price stays controlled and does not overpromise a brand before stock, size and route are checked.

## All-in answer

It is an all-in guide for the selected extension package scope: normal structure, wall, roof, tile, ceiling, basic door/window allowance, basic M&E allowance, paint and cleanup. Add-ons and special items are listed separately.
