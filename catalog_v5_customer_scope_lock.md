# Catalog V5 Customer Scope Lock

Public catalog rule: describe specification, material tier, size allowance and included quantity. Do not list brand names unless the final quotation intentionally lists a brand/model.

## Floor tiles
- Basic: economy ceramic floor tile.
- Standard: better ceramic or entry-level vitrified floor tile.
- Better Finish: upgraded vitrified or approved premium-category floor tile.
- Include suitable bedding or adhesive and grout.
- Include tile skirting about 4 inch / 100mm to new plastered wall areas unless wall is fully tiled, blocked by cabinet/table top or stated otherwise.
- Exact tile size and RM material allowance must be locked later from the materials list.

## Ceiling
- Material: plaster ceiling board.
- Basic: plain flat plaster ceiling board.
- Standard: flat plaster ceiling board with smoother joint treatment and access panel where required.
- Better Finish: simple design plaster ceiling may be included, such as tray, L-box or feature ceiling in agreed area. LED strip, cove light and complex design are included only if stated.

## Door and window
- Normal enclosed extension may include one standard single-leaf door set up to about 3ft x 7ft where layout requires it.
- Normal enclosed extension may include one aluminium framed window allowance.
- Basic window allowance: up to about 4ft x 3ft.
- Standard window allowance: up to about 5ft x 4ft.
- Better Finish window allowance: up to about 6ft x 4ft.
- Sliding, folding, full-height glass, feature facade, special glass and extra openings are quoted separately unless listed.

## Electrical
For a normal extension area up to about 150 sqft:
- Basic: up to 1 light point with switch and 2 x 13A socket points.
- Standard: up to 2 light points with switch and 3 x 13A socket points.
- Better Finish: up to 3 light/downlight points with switching and 4 x 13A socket points.

Actual fitting model, water-heater point, hood/hob/cooker point, air-conditioner point, new circuit, DB upgrade and earthing correction are quoted separately unless listed.

## Plumbing
For back/kitchen/yard/laundry extensions only:
- Basic: up to 1 cold-water point, 1 waste outlet and 1 floor trap if practical route is near existing line.
- Standard: Basic plumbing plus 1 washing-machine point or outdoor tap where listed.
- Better Finish: Standard plumbing plus 1 utility-sink or outdoor-tap point where listed.

Hot-water route, manhole, sewer, soil stack and major drainage reroute are custom quote unless listed.

## Bathroom add-on
Basic bathroom:
- 1 standard two-piece close-coupled WC set.
- 1 standard bidet spray.
- 1 wall-hung or pedestal basin.
- 1 basin tap and trap.
- 1 cold-water shower set.
- 1 floor trap and waste route.
- Waterproofing to bathroom floor and wet-wall/upturn coverage stated in quotation.
- Economy ceramic bathroom floor tile.
- Wall tile to agreed wet-wall height.
- 1 toilet door set up to about 2.5ft x 7ft.
- 1 light point with switch.

Standard bathroom:
- 1 better-grade two-piece close-coupled WC set.
- Better tap, basin and shower allowance where listed.
- Better waterproofing and tile allowance.
- Exhaust-fan point may be included where listed.

Better Finish bathroom:
- 1 premium one-piece WC combo set where available within selected allowance.
- Upgraded tap, basin, shower, tile and accessory allowance where listed.
- Wall tile may be agreed height or near/full height where listed.

Water heater, shower screen, LED mirror, premium mirror cabinet, major sewer/manhole work and long concealed pipe routes are excluded unless listed.

## Wet kitchen
Wet Kitchen Set A/B/C may include:
- concrete/tiled table top for selected straight-run length;
- sink opening when sink size is confirmed;
- 1 standard kitchen sink where listed;
- 1 standard kitchen tap where listed;
- cold-water, waste and drainage connection to practical nearby route.

Base cabinet, wall cabinet, aluminium cabinet, quartz/granite/solid-surface top, hood, hob, appliances and premium sink/tap are quoted separately unless listed.

## Paint
- Surface preparation to new work area.
- Sealer/primer where required.
- Two coats emulsion paint to new internal walls.
- Ceiling paint where ceiling is included.
- Exterior-suitable paint to exposed new external walls.
- Better Finish may use premium paint range where listed.

## Customer answer style
Answer customer questions by specification and tier, not brand. Final quotation must confirm exact size, model, quantity, route and upgrade charge.
