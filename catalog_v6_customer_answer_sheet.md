# Catalog V6 Customer Answer Sheet

Use this sheet when customer asks what is included. Answer by specification and tier, not by brand. Brand/model is confirmed only in the official quotation.

## Basic package answer

Basic is for normal extension with practical finish. It includes structure, walling, roof, floor tile, plaster ceiling board, one normal door/window allowance where layout requires it, basic electrical points, basic wet-area plumbing where applicable, paint, waste removal and final sweep-clean.

Typical items:

- economy ceramic floor tile;
- tile skirting about 4 inch / 100mm;
- plain flat plaster ceiling board;
- one standard single-leaf door set up to about 3ft x 7ft where layout requires it;
- one standard aluminium framed window up to about 4ft x 3ft where layout requires it;
- up to 1 light point with switch and 2 x 13A socket points for normal extension up to about 150 sqft;
- back/kitchen/yard/laundry only: up to 1 cold-water point, 1 waste outlet and 1 floor trap if route is practical;
- surface preparation, sealer/primer where required and two coats emulsion paint.

## Standard package answer

Standard improves the finish and allowance. It keeps the same structure basis but upgrades tile tier, ceiling finish, electrical point allowance and practical wet-area allowance.

Typical items:

- better ceramic or entry-level vitrified floor tile;
- tile skirting about 4 inch / 100mm;
- flat plaster ceiling board with smoother joint treatment and access panel where required;
- one standard single-leaf door set where layout requires it;
- one aluminium framed window up to about 5ft x 4ft where layout requires it;
- up to 2 light points with switch and 3 x 13A socket points for normal extension up to about 150 sqft;
- back/kitchen/yard/laundry only: Basic plumbing plus 1 washing-machine point or outdoor tap where listed;
- smoother finishing and standard paint range by quotation.

## Better Finish package answer

Better Finish is for upgraded finishing. It keeps the same structure basis but improves visible finish, tile tier, ceiling design option, point allowance and bathroom/wet-area allowance.

Typical items:

- upgraded vitrified or approved premium-category floor tile;
- tile skirting about 4 inch / 100mm;
- simple design plaster ceiling may be included, such as tray, L-box or feature ceiling in agreed area;
- one upgraded door set/finish where listed;
- one larger or upgraded aluminium framed window up to about 6ft x 4ft where layout requires it;
- up to 3 light/downlight points with switching and 4 x 13A socket points for normal extension up to about 150 sqft;
- back/kitchen/yard/laundry only: Standard plumbing plus one utility-sink or outdoor-tap point where listed;
- premium paint range or upgraded finish where listed.

## Bathroom answers

Basic bathroom:

- standard two-piece close-coupled WC set;
- standard bidet spray;
- wall-hung or pedestal basin;
- basin tap and trap;
- cold-water shower set;
- floor trap and waste route;
- waterproofing to floor and wet-wall/upturn coverage stated in quotation;
- economy ceramic bathroom floor tile;
- wall tile to agreed wet-wall height;
- toilet door set up to about 2.5ft x 7ft;
- 1 light point with switch.

Standard bathroom:

- better-grade two-piece close-coupled WC set;
- better tap, basin and shower allowance where listed;
- better waterproofing and tile allowance;
- exhaust-fan point may be included where listed.

Better Finish bathroom:

- premium one-piece WC combo set where available within selected allowance;
- upgraded tap, basin, shower, tile and accessory allowance where listed;
- wall tile may be agreed height or near/full height where listed;
- exhaust-fan point may be included where listed.

Excluded unless listed: water heater, shower screen, LED mirror, premium mirror cabinet, premium vanity cabinet, premium accessories, major sewer/manhole relocation, soil-stack relocation and long concealed pipe route.

## Wet kitchen answers

Wet Kitchen Set A/B/C may include concrete/tiled table top for selected straight-run length, sink opening, 1 standard kitchen sink, 1 standard kitchen tap and cold-water/waste/drainage connection to practical nearby route when listed.

Do not tell customer that cabinet is included unless the official quotation says so. Base cabinet, wall cabinet, aluminium cabinet, quartz, granite, solid surface, hood, hob, appliances, premium sink/tap and long concealed plumbing route are quoted separately.

## What to say when customer asks for brand

Use this wording:

`Brand/model will be confirmed in the official quotation. The catalog locks the item specification and allowance tier first, so the price remains controlled and we do not overpromise a brand before checking stock, size and route.`

## What to say when customer asks if it is all-in

Use this wording:

`It is an all-in guide for the selected extension package scope, including normal structure, wall, roof, tile, ceiling, basic door/window allowance, basic M&E allowance, paint and cleanup. Add-ons and special items like cabinet, premium glass, shower screen, water heater, DB upgrade, sewer/manhole work and council/professional fees are listed separately.`
