## Owner scope decisions applied

The V5 catalogs were updated after owner confirmation that the packages should read closer to standard homeowner renovation/all-in scope, with material and finish tier differences between Basic, Standard and Better Finish.

Applied updates:

- plaster ceiling board is now specified;
- Basic ceiling is plain flat plaster ceiling board, not open roof;
- Standard ceiling is flat plaster ceiling board with smoother finish/access-panel allowance where required;
- Better Finish may include simple design plaster ceiling such as tray/L-box/feature ceiling where listed;
- tile skirting is included at about 4 inch / 100mm to new plastered wall areas, subject to exclusions;
- normal door and aluminium window allowances are now included for normal enclosed extensions;
- electrical quantities are set by package for a normal extension area up to about 150 sqft;
- plumbing quantities are set for back/kitchen/yard/laundry extensions where route is practical;
- Basic/Standard/Better bathroom WC tiers are separated into two-piece and one-piece combo treatment;
- toilet door allowance is specified by typical size;
- wet kitchen sink/tap inclusion is clarified for Wet Kitchen Set A/B/C when listed;
- wet kitchen cabinets remain quote-only unless specifically listed;
- paint system now states preparation, sealer/primer where required and two coats.
