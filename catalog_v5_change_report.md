## Key contradiction handling

- **Floor tiles:** V5 confirms the supported tile categories but requires the official quotation to state tile size and RM material allowance.
- **Tile skirting:** Excluded unless the official quotation states area, height and tile category.
- **Ceiling:** Basic has no ceiling/underside lining; Standard and Better Finish ceiling areas and technical details must be explicitly stated.
- **Doors and windows:** Main packages include formation and making good of normal-sized openings only unless a complete door/window system is specifically listed.
- **Wet kitchen:** The guide means concrete/tiled table top and preparation; cabinets, sink, tap, premium countertop, hood, hob and appliances are separately defined.
- **Bathroom WC:** One WC set is included, but one-piece/two-piece type, brand and model remain subject to official quotation.
- **Electrical:** No impractical point-per-square-foot formula is used. Exact quantities and wiring method must be listed.
- **Plumbing:** Laundry rough-in and other water/waste points are separated from the main extension scope unless listed.
- **Paint:** Supported coat guidance is stated, while primer/sealer, exterior system and door/frame coating remain quotation-specific.
