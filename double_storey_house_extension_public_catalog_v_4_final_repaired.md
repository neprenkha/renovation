# 01. HOW TO USE THIS CATALOG

1. Choose your work type:
   - Ground floor only
   - Upper floor add-on only
   - Full ground + upper extension

2. Choose your extension area:
   - Back / kitchen / yard / laundry area
   - Front / porch / facade-sensitive area

3. Measure the rough size:
   - For ground floor only: length x width = sqft
   - For upper floor add-on only: length x width = sqft
   - For full ground + upper: length x width x 2 floors = total built-up sqft

4. Choose package:
   - Basic
   - Standard
   - Better Finish

5. Check the **Ready-Calculated Common Size Examples** first.

6. Add any extra items required:
   - Wet kitchen / table top
   - Toilet / bathroom
   - Laundry
   - Electrical / plumbing
   - Aluminium / glass / grille / cabinet

7. Send photos/video, rough size, house type and location for checking.

# 11. IMPORTANT SITE REVIEW NOTES

## 1. Property Type Notice

Prices are based on normal terrace/link-house conditions.

Semi-D, corner lot, bungalow, cluster home, gated/guarded property, exposed side wall, larger roof edge, side drainage, high ceiling, special facade or irregular layout may require site review and price adjustment.

Bungalow / link bungalow is generally custom quotation.

---

## 2. Existing Wall Type Notice

Package price assumes normal brick/block wall hacking only.

Concrete wall, RC wall, precast wall, IBS wall, structural wall, load-bearing wall or large wall opening requires site review and custom quotation.

---

## 3. Structure Suitability Notice

Upper-floor add-on and full ground + upper extension require structure/site review.

Existing structure, footing, beam, column, roofline, cracks, settlement, water leakage and previous renovation condition can affect final price.

---

## 4. Normal Height Assumption

Price assumes normal residential wall height and normal extension roof height.

High ceiling, double-volume space, special roof pitch or exposed high wall is quoted separately.

---

## 5. Opening Limitation

Package rates assume normal door/window openings only.

Large openings, sliding door, folding door, full-height glass, balcony glass, heavy aluminium/glass facade and grille/gate are quoted separately.

---

## 6. Roof / Gutter / Downpipe

Gutter/downpipe is included only where a new roof edge is created and stated in the quotation.

Existing rainwater reroute, low drain level, complicated discharge route, old roof leakage or existing roof defect is quoted separately.

Upper-floor roofline changes and front facade roofline work require site review.

---

## 7. Site Access

Prices assume normal access and normal material delivery route.

No rear access, narrow lane, long carry distance, guarded estate restriction, limited working hours, upper-floor material lifting or no storage space may affect final quotation.

---

## 8. Waste Disposal

Basic construction waste removal is for the agreed work area only.

Major hacking waste, skip bin, old furniture, existing cabinet removal, unrelated rubbish or extra disposal is quoted separately.

---

## 9. Minimum Job Price

For very small works, minimum mobilization price applies.

The ready-calculated common size table may override simple sqft multiplication.

Below 50 sqft requires site review / minimum job price.

---

## 10. Owner-Supplied Items

Owner-supplied items are subject to suitability check.

Installation, missing parts, modification, warranty and compatibility issues are not included unless stated.

---

## 11. Timeline

Work duration will be stated in the official quotation after site review.

Rain, curing time, material lead time, authority/management approval and site condition may affect schedule.

---

## 12. Payment

Payment schedule will be stated in the official quotation.

Variation or additional work must be confirmed separately.

---

## 13. Warranty

Workmanship warranty / defect liability period will be stated in the official quotation.

Warranty does not cover existing house movement, old leakage, existing crack, owner-supplied product defect, third-party damage, misuse or work outside agreed scope.
