# 01. HOW TO USE THIS CATALOG

1. Choose work type: ground floor only, upper floor add-on only or full ground + upper extension.
2. Choose area: back/kitchen/yard/laundry or front/porch/facade-sensitive area.
3. Measure rough size. For full double-storey: length x width x 2 floors = total built-up sqft.
4. Choose package: Basic, Standard or Better Finish.
5. Check common-size tables first. Use sqft band only if size is not listed.
6. Add wet kitchen, toilet, laundry, electrical, plumbing, aluminium/glass, grille, cabinet, balcony or staircase if needed.
7. Send location, house type, rough size, photos/video and known site issues for checking.

# 10. SITE REVIEW NOTES

Prices assume normal terrace/link-house, normal height, normal access, normal brick/block wall hacking and normal delivery route. Semi-D, corner lot, bungalow, cluster home, gated property, high ceiling, exposed side wall, special facade, no rear access, narrow lane, concrete/RC/precast/IBS wall, structural wall, low drain, old leakage, cracks, old renovation defect, upper-floor lifting issue or management restriction may require price adjustment or custom quote.

Below 50 sqft requires site review/minimum job price. Work duration, payment schedule and workmanship warranty are stated in the official quotation.
