## Customer-facing improvements

V5 clarifies:

- Basic, Standard and Better Finish package differences;
- floor tile category by tier;
- tile skirting inclusion around 4 inch / 100mm;
- plaster ceiling board scope;
- Better Finish simple design ceiling option;
- door allowance up to approx. 3ft x 7ft;
- toilet door allowance up to approx. 2.5ft x 7ft;
- aluminium window allowance by tier;
- electrical point allowance by tier;
- plumbing allowance by work area and tier;
- bathroom/WC tier differences;
- wet kitchen table top, sink and tap scope;
- cabinet and premium-top exclusions;
- paint system basis;
- double-storey upper-floor structure and plumbing review notes.

## Brand rule

V5 does not promise brand names. It describes item specification, size allowance, material tier, included quantity and quote-only exclusions. Brand/model should be listed only in the official quotation when selected.
